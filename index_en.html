About Me

Welcome to my professional space. I am an Infrastructure Engineer with a passion for computer science that dates back to when I was 11 years old. My academic background at Federico II University and my practical experience have allowed me to develop cross-disciplinary skills in the fields of IT and infrastructure engineering.

I combine technical programming knowledge with the ability to analyze and design infrastructures. I am particularly interested in virtualization technologies (VMware, Veeam, Proxmox), cybersecurity (Fortinet), artificial intelligence (OpenAI, Anthropic, NVIDIA), and data analysis with ETL solutions, areas in which I have gained significant professional experience.

Since 1998, I have served as the Chief Information Officer (CIO) at Sipos Srl, where I have led the company's digital transformation and implemented innovative solutions to optimize business processes.

As a member of Gartner Peer Insight, I provide reviews and analyses for major industry players such as Fortinet, VMware, and Veeam, contributing to the evaluation and evolution of emerging technologies.

Beyond information technology, I nurture a deep passion for physics and mathematics. These disciplines not only enrich my analytical approach to technical problems but also serve as a continuous source of inspiration for the innovative solutions I develop in my daily work.
