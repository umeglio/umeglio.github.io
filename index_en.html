About Me

Welcome to my professional space. I am an Infrastructure Engineer with a passion for computer science that dates back to the age of 11. My academic background at Federico II University and my hands-on experience have allowed me to develop cross-disciplinary skills in the fields of computer science and infrastructure.

I combine technical programming knowledge with the ability to analyze and design infrastructure. I am particularly interested in virtualization technologies (VMware, Veeam, Proxmox), cybersecurity (Fortinet), artificial intelligence (OpenAI, Anthropic, NVIDIA) and data analysis with ETL solutions, areas in which I have gained significant professional experience.

Since 1998, I have held the role of CIO at Sipos Srl, where I have led the company's digital transformation and implemented innovative solutions to optimize business processes.

As a member of Gartner Peer Insight, I provide reviews and analyses for major industry players such as Fortinet, VMware and Veeam, contributing to the evaluation and evolution of emerging technologies.

Beyond computer science, I have a deep passion for physics and mathematics. These disciplines not only enrich my analytical approach to technical problems, but also serve as a continuous source of inspiration for the innovative solutions I develop in my daily work.
